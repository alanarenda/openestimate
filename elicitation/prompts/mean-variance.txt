## Available Distribution Types

You may choose from:
- **Normal** (Gaussian): For variables that can be positive or negative, symmetric around the mean
- **Lognormal**: For strictly positive variables, often right-skewed (e.g., prices, sizes, counts)
- **Beta**: For variables bounded between 0 and 1 (e.g., proportions, probabilities)
